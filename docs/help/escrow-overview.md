# Escrow & Compliance Overview

The Possible Tackle Exchange routes every payment into an escrow ledger until
orders are marked as fulfilled or refunded. Sellers onboard through Stripe
Connect and accept the Seller Trust & Safety policy as part of activation.

1. Buyer submits payment. A hold is placed for the listing price + fees.
2. Seller receives a shipment checklist and label guidance in the dashboard.
3. Funds are released after delivery confirmation or manual review.
4. Disputes pause payouts and trigger compliance workflows.
